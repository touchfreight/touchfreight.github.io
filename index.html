About Us

Our Story

Our product started as a class project at the University of California, Berkeley. As part of the University's Mobile Entrepreneurship Challenge, we spent thirteen weeks designing a prototype to pitch to a panel of investors and industry leaders.

We have spoken to numerous carrier companies including one of our co-founder's parents, who own a freight forwarding firm, and learned that there is a severe lack of good technology available to shippers. We plan to change that by providing affordable and easy-to-use, yet truly powerful solutions for our clients. As young software developers, we can bring a new perspective to your business and directly help you increase your bottom line.
